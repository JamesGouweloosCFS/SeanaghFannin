Purpose

Carrick Wealth uses cookies to provide you with a more personalized, responsive service and to ensure that our website functions effectively. This Cookie Notice forms part of our Privacy Notice. 

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, remember your preferences (such as location, language, and display settings), and improve your browsing experience.

What types of cookies do we use?

We use the following categories of cookies:

Strictly necessary cookies-required for the website to function (e.g. security, log-in, navigation).
Performance and analytics cookies-help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it (e.g. Google Analytics, Adobe).
Functional cookies-remember your preferences and settings to make your visits smoother.
Advertising cookies-may be used (with your consent) to deliver more relevant ads through providers such as Google Adwords.
Cookies may be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (remain until expiry or deletion).

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies to:

recognize your device and save you re-entering information;
analyze which pages and features are most popular;
ensure the website operates securely and efficiently;
improve our marketing and communications.
